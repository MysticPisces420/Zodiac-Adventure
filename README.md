## Game Vision
**Zodiac-Adventure** is an ambitious, mythology-driven 3D action/adventure game that brings the 12 zodiac signs to life. Players embark on an educational journey through 12 mystical realms, each faithfully representing the lore and characteristics of its corresponding zodiac sign. With real-time hybrid combat, exploration-focused gameplay, and both single-player and multiplayer modes, Zodiac-Adventure offers an immersive experience that is both entertaining and educational.

## Core Features
- **12 Playable Zodiac Characters**: Each with unique supernatural abilities rooted in their zodiac mythology
- **12 Mythologically-Accurate Realms**: Fully realized worlds representing each zodiac sign
- **Hybrid Real-Time Combat**: Dynamic action combat with tactical elements
- **Exploration & Story-Driven Narrative**: Rich lore and character development
- **Single-Player & Multiplayer Modes**: Experience the adventure solo or with friends
- **Educational Content**: Learn authentic zodiac mythology and cultural significance
- **Dynamic Abilities**: Supernatural powers tied to zodiac characteristics

## Gameplay Mechanics

### Combat System
- **Real-Time Hybrid Combat**: Blend of action and tactical decision-making
- **Zodiac-Based Abilities**: Each character has unique abilities tied to their sign
- **Enemy Types**: Creatures and enemies specific to each realm

### Exploration
- **Open-World Realms**: Freely explore each zodiac realm
- **Environmental Puzzles**: Solve challenges based on zodiac lore
- **Hidden Secrets**: Discover lore, artifacts, and special abilities

### Story & Lore
- **Character-Driven Narrative**: Deep character backstories and development
- **Mythological Accuracy**: Faithful representation of zodiac mythology
- **Educational Elements**: Learn about zodiac origins, mythology, and cultural significance

### Multiplayer Features
- **Co-op Exploration**: Team up to explore realms together
- **Multiplayer Challenges**: Special events and battles
- **Trading & Social Systems**: Interact with other players
