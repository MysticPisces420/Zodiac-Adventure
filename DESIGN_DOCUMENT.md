# Game Design Document for Zodiac Adventure

## Game Overview
Zodiac Adventure is an engaging role-playing game (RPG) that immerses players in a fantastical world inspired by astrological symbols and mythological paradigms. Players will explore different realms, engage in combat, and enhance their knowledge of astrology through interactive gameplay.

## Core Mechanics
- **Exploration:** Players can traverse various realms representing different zodiac signs.
- **Combat:** Turn-based combat system providing strategic depth.
- **Quests:** Side quests and main quests that enhance the storyline and gameplay experience.

## Character System
- **Character Creation:** Players can create a character aligned with a zodiac sign, which influences abilities and skills.
- **Stats:** Each character has stats that include health, attack, defense, and special abilities.
- **Skill Trees:** Players can unlock unique abilities based on their zodiac alignment.

## Realm Design
- **Zodiac Realms:** Each realm is themed after a zodiac sign and includes unique challenges and visuals.
- **Environment:** Dynamic environments that change based on the playerâ€™s actions and time.

## Combat System
- **Turn-Based Mechanics:** Players take turns attacking, using items, or utilizing special skills.
- **Combo System:** Encourage chaining attacks for increased damage and effects.
- **Enemy Types:** Distinct enemies for each realm with unique abilities and difficulties.

## Progression System
- **Experience Points:** Gain experience for battles, quests, and exploration.
- **Leveling Up:** Increasing character stats and unlocking new abilities as players progress.
- **Achievements:** In-game achievements that encourage exploration and skill mastery.

## Multiplayer Features
- **Co-op Mode:** Players can team up to complete quests together.
- **Competitive Events:** Seasonal events where players can compete for rewards.

## Educational Framework
- **Astrology Learning:** Incorporate educational content about astrology through quests and character interactions.
- **Interactive Elements:** Games, quizzes, and challenges related to zodiac signs and astrological knowledge.

## Technical Specifications
- **Platform:** PC, Console, Mobile
- **Engine:** Unity or Unreal Engine, depending on the final design choices.
- **Networking:** Support for online multiplayer and cloud saving features.
